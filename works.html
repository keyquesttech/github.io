Salt cellar

This triangular salt cellar is specifically designed to accommodate the "perfect pinch"—the action of using three fingers to pick up a consistent amount of salt. The unique shape not only adds aesthetic appeal but also enhances the user's experience by making it easier to grab just the right amount of seasoning.

Enclave prototype

The concept behind Enclave is akin to a "territory within a territory"—it's a macropad designed to enhance your computing experience by seamlessly integrating additional ports. Targeted at enthusiast users, Enclave aims to expand the functionality of a conventional shortcut keyboard by offering the added benefit of extra connectivity, all through a device that feels familiar yet innovative. This first prototype features a two-part plastic case and a custom PCB, laying the groundwork for what promises to be a versatile and user-friendly tool.

Enclave-1

Enclave-1 represents the final, polished product, arrived at after multiple iterations underwent market testing to identify the most commercially viable option. The device incorporates a custom-made PCB, a specially designed case, and adhesive feet, culminating in a tailored solution that stands out in both functionality and user experience. Rtv6 - Revo to V6

The Revo to V6 is a 3D printer nozzle adapter designed to bridge the gap between E3D's proprietary Revo system and the world's most commonly used nozzle size, V6. Successfully crowdfunded on Indiegogo, the product is now available in select online stores and operates under a licensing agreement with Mellow 3D. Remarkably, all of these milestones were achieved by me, Matias, in under 60 days.
